College Research

Our research ranges from the core disciplines of our schools through to interdisciplinary areas. 

Multidisciplinary research themes bring our disciplines together to provide new insights and solutions in a range of topics. They attract funding from a wide range of bodies, especially UK and EU research councils. 

You can find more information about our research within each school, research institute or research theme.
