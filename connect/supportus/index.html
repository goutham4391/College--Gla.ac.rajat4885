Privacy notice 

Your support, big or small, has the potential to help Glasgow change the world.

Our students, staff, researchers and alumni community have been changing the world since 1451. Support from people like you has helped us to tackle global problems, from pioneering treatments for cancer, heart disease and stroke, to tackling inequalities and revolutionising technology.

At Glasgow, we believe people should be at the heart of everything we do. We are committed, with your support, to ensuring all staff and students reach their full potential and continue to make extraordinary achievements. We are dedicated to supporting life-changing scholarships, to investing in innovative learning environments and to helping fund ground breaking research.

Whether you are giving your time, leaving a gift in your Will, making a regular or one-off gift, we have a project to inspire you.

Support today, help Glasgow change the world tomorrow.
