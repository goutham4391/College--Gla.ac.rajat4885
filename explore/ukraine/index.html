Aid for Ukraine

Our donation hub closed on  Friday 20 May, having delivered around 1,000 boxes of humanitrian aid to Kyiv, Poltava and Kharkiv.

Friday 20 June was our World Refugee Day Ceremony.  Sunflower seedlings originally planted at a vigil we held in May, were replanted to symbolise solidarity with Ukraine and all those seeking sanctuary.
