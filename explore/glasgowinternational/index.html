International news

  • 09 Sep

    UofG Professor elected to prestigious Academy of Europe

    An academic from the University of Glasgow has been elected to one of Europe’s foremost research bodies, the Academia Europaea (Academy of Europe).
  • 29 Sep

    Scottish households urged to join search for Australian children’s artworks

    The global search for hundreds of precious artworks created by Australian First Nations children who were forcibly taken from their families in the 1940s has reached Scotland.
  • 24 Oct

    Science sleuths solve century-old mystery of Martian meteorite’s discovery

    A toxin which makes pigs vomit is the surprising key which has unlocked the century-old mystery of the origins of a Martian meteorite, and the possible identity of the Black student who discovered it.
  • 01 Sep

    New £7 million NIHR funding for global health research

    Researchers at the University of Glasgow have been awarded a multi-million pound grant, to establish a new health research unit to help tackle health inequalities in Latin America
