Introduction to Student Support Officers

 

 

Our UofG International Welcome Team are on hand to help answer all of your questions at the start of term regarding your arrival and settling into Glasgow. The team are here to support you with any questions you have until you're underway with your studies.

Ask us about how to register, getting to campus, shopping, getting to know Glasgow, BRP collection, Orientation activities and more.

Our team are all UofG current/recently graduated students and look forward to meeting you!
